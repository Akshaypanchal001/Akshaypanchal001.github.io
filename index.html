PCB Design Services

1. Core Design Capabilities Schematic Capture & Entry: Conversion of concept sketches or legacy diagrams into industry-standard schematic formats. PCB Layout & Routing: Precise component placement and manual routing for optimal performance. Multi-Layer Expertise: Design capability ranging from simple 2-layer boards to complex 30+ layer rigid-flex designs. Library Management: Creation and maintenance of IPC-compliant component libraries (symbols, footprints, and 3D models). Reverse Engineering: Generating design files (Gerbers, Schematics) from physical legacy boards. 2. Advanced Technical Expertise High-Speed Digital Design: Impedance control, differential pair routing, length matching (DDR3/4/5, PCIe, USB 3.0+). RF & Microwave Design: Antenna integration, RF shielding, and specialized material selection (Rogers, Teflon). HDI (High-Density Interconnect): Blind/buried vias, micro-vias, and via-in-pad technology for miniaturized electronics. Power Electronics: Heavy copper designs for high-current applications, thermal management for power supplies, and creepage/clearance analysis. Rigid-Flex & Flex Circuits: 3D folding simulation and bend-radius optimization for wearables and compact devices. Signal Integrity (SI) & Power Integrity (PI) Analysis: Pre- and post-layout simulation to prevent crosstalk, reflections, and voltage drops. 3. Design for Excellence (DFX) DFM (Design for Manufacturing): Optimization to reduce fabrication costs and yield loss (e.g., acid trap removal, aspect ratio checks). DFA (Design for Assembly): Ensuring components are placed for efficient automated assembly (pick-and-place) and soldering. DFT (Design for Test): Implementation of test points and JTAG boundary scan integration for mass production testing. 4. Industry Standards & Compliance IPC Standards: Designs compliant with IPC-2221 (Generic Design) and IPC-7351 (Land Patterns). Regulatory Pre-Compliance: Design practices to pass EMC/EMI (FCC, CE), UL safety, and RoHS/REACH environmental standards. Sector-Specific Standards: ISO 13485 (Medical), DO-254 (Aerospace), or ISO 26262 (Automotive) compliance. 5. Turnkey & Support Services Rapid Prototyping: Quick-turn design and low-volume fabrication support. BOM Management: Component sourcing, obsolescence checking, and cross-reference selection for cost reduction. Fabrication & Assembly Support: Liaison with manufacturing houses to resolve EQ (Engineering Questions) instantly. 6. Standard Deliverables Package Manufacturing Files: Gerber X2/RS-274X, ODB++, NC Drill files. Assembly Files: Pick & Place (Centroid) data, Assembly Drawings. Documentation: 3D Step models, PDF Schematics, Stack-up Report, and Impedance Tables. "Why Choose Us?" (Value Proposition) First-Pass Success: Commitment to error-free designs that work the first time, reducing prototyping spins. Cost-Optimized Design: Selecting components and layer structures that balance performance with manufacturing budget. Scalable Solutions: Designs ready for seamless transition from prototype to mass production.
