PCB Design Services

1. Core Design Capabilities

  • Schematic Capture & Entry: Conversion of concept sketches or legacy diagrams into industry-standard schematic formats.
  • PCB Layout & Routing: Precise component placement and manual routing for optimal performance.
  • Multi-Layer Expertise: Design capability ranging from simple 2-layer boards to complex 30+ layer rigid-flex designs.
  • Library Management: Creation and maintenance of IPC-compliant component libraries (symbols, footprints, and 3D models).
  • Reverse Engineering: Generating design files (Gerbers, Schematics) from physical legacy boards.

2. Advanced Technical Expertise

  • High-Speed Digital Design: Impedance control, differential pair routing, and length matching (DDR3/4/5, PCIe, USB 3.0+).
  • RF & Microwave Design: Antenna integration, RF shielding, and specialized material selection (Rogers, Teflon).
  • HDI (High-Density Interconnect): Blind/buried vias, micro-vias, and via-in-pad technology.
  • Power Electronics: Heavy copper designs, thermal management, creepage and clearance analysis.
  • Rigid-Flex & Flex Circuits: Bend-radius optimization and 3D folding considerations.
  • SI / PI Analysis: Pre- and post-layout simulation to prevent crosstalk and voltage drop.

3. Design for Excellence (DFX)

  • DFM: Fabrication-ready designs to reduce cost and yield loss.
  • DFA: Assembly-optimized placement for automated production.
  • DFT: Test point strategy and boundary scan integration.

4. Industry Standards & Compliance

  • IPC Standards: IPC-2221 and IPC-7351 compliant designs.
  • Regulatory Readiness: EMC/EMI, UL, RoHS, REACH.
  • Sector Standards: ISO 13485, ISO 26262, DO-254.

5. Turnkey & Support Services

  • Rapid Prototyping: Quick-turn design and low-volume support.
  • BOM Management: Sourcing, alternates, and obsolescence checks.
  • Manufacturing Support: Direct coordination with fabrication and assembly partners.

6. Standard Deliverables Package

  • Gerber X2 / RS-274X, ODB++, NC Drill files
  • Pick & Place data, Assembly drawings
  • PDF schematics, 3D STEP, stack-up and impedance reports

  • Cost-Optimized Design: Performance balanced with manufacturing cost.

PCB CAM Engineering & Manufacturing Services

CoreCircuits delivers an end-to-end solution for printed circuit boards, seamlessly transitioning designs from front-end engineering to high-precision fabrication. Every board is optimized for yield, performance, and cost before entering production.

1. Core Capabilities

  • Front-End CAM Engineering: Comprehensive DFM audits, netlist verification, and etch compensation to eliminate manufacturing risks.
  • Precision Fabrication: Capabilities ranging from simple prototypes to complex HDI, rigid-flex, and heavy copper boards (up to 10oz).
  • Impedance Control: Advanced stack-up modeling using Polar Si9000 to ensure signal integrity for high-speed designs.
  • Panelization & Tooling: Optimized array layouts (V-Score / Tab-Route) and stencil generation to streamline downstream assembly.

2. Technical Specifications at a Glance

  • Layer Count: Single-sided boards to complex 30+ layer constructions.
  • Materials: FR4, Rogers, Polyimide, Aluminum Core substrates.
  • Surface Finishes: ENIG, HASL (Lead-Free), Immersion Silver, Immersion Tin, Hard Gold.
  • Quality Assurance: 100% Electrical Testing (Flying Probe), AOI inspection, and cross-section analysis.
