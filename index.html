PCB Design Services

At CoreCircuits, we deliver end-to-end PCB design solutions engineered for performance, reliability, and manufacturability.

1. Core Design Capabilities

  • Schematic Capture & Entry: Conversion of concept sketches or legacy diagrams into industry-standard schematic formats.
  • PCB Layout & Routing: Precise component placement and manual routing for optimal electrical and thermal performance.
  • Multi-Layer Expertise: Design capability from simple 2-layer boards to complex 30+ layer rigid-flex designs.
  • Library Management: Creation and maintenance of IPC-compliant symbols, footprints, and 3D models.
  • Reverse Engineering: Generation of Gerbers and schematics from physical legacy boards.

2. Advanced Technical Expertise

  • High-Speed Digital Design: Impedance control, differential pair routing, and length matching (DDR3/4/5, PCIe, USB 3.0+).
  • RF & Microwave Design: Antenna integration, RF shielding, and material selection (Rogers, Teflon).
  • HDI Design: Blind/buried vias, microvias, and via-in-pad technology for compact electronics.
  • Power Electronics: Heavy copper layouts, thermal management, and creepage/clearance analysis.
  • Rigid-Flex & Flex Circuits: Bend-radius optimization and 3D folding considerations.
  • SI / PI Analysis: Pre- and post-layout simulations to prevent crosstalk, reflections, and voltage drop.

3. Design for Excellence (DFX)

  • DFM: Fabrication-ready designs to improve yield and reduce cost.
  • DFA: Optimized component placement for automated assembly.
  • DFT: Test point insertion and boundary scan (JTAG) for production testing.

4. Industry Standards & Compliance

  • IPC Standards: IPC-2221, IPC-7351 compliant designs.
  • Regulatory Pre-Compliance: EMC/EMI (FCC, CE), UL safety, RoHS / REACH readiness.
  • Sector-Specific Standards: ISO 13485 (Medical), DO-254 (Aerospace), ISO 26262 (Automotive).

5. Turnkey & Support Services

  • Rapid Prototyping: Quick-turn design and low-volume fabrication support.
  • BOM Management: Component sourcing, obsolescence checks, and cost optimization.
  • Fabrication & Assembly Support: Direct coordination with manufacturers to resolve EQs.

6. Standard Deliverables Package

  • Manufacturing Files: Gerber X2 / RS-274X, ODB++, NC Drill.
  • Assembly Files: Pick & Place data, assembly drawings.
  • Documentation: 3D STEP models, PDF schematics, stack-up reports, impedance tables.

Why Choose CoreCircuits?

  • First-Pass Success: Designs engineered to work right the first time.
  • Cost-Optimized Design: Balanced performance and manufacturing cost.
  • Scalable Solutions: Designs ready for smooth transition from prototype to mass production.
